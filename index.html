About us

e-Pradarshanini is an online platform used to exhibit the projects performed by the Computer Engineering department.

Prakalp is the summarization of the projects in a nutshell and forms the core of the efforts put in by the faculty and students as a team towards innovation and research .

The major areas in which Computer department specializes include
1. AI, Machine Learning and deep learning
2. Data warehousing and Big data analytics
3. IOT and Robotics
4.Image Processing and Multimedia
5.Networking and Security (Block Chain)
6.High Performance Computing
7. Cloud Computing

In the year 2020, the department is proud of its 114 projects that have led to more than 90 research publications in International Journals and Conferences of repute and around 36 awards in various competitions. All of this is highlighted through our yearly booklet Prakalp.
